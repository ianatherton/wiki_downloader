I've been playing Crawl on CBR2 since August 2023. In late November and early December 2023, I played on CKO due to issues with CBR2. I'm now playing 0.32 trunk. Here's my stats page. (technically not the official one, but it has more info than what CAO has)

Tournaments

This section will document my performance in DCSS tournaments. For each tournament, info will be in the following bullet points:

  • Tournament Page: This contains a link to the tournament page.
  • Tournament Profile: The tournament page has a profile for each player, so I'll link to mine here.
  • Last Updated: This only applies to a tournament in progress. The idea is that info on this page is accurate at the time of last update, but it may be outdated by now. The time is in CET because that's how the wiki is configured to interpret ~~~~~.
  • Score (Rank): My score for the tournament with overall rank in parentheses. Unlike the tournament page, ties are not compressed here.
  • Characters: My characters in each tournament will be documented as sub-bullets. Early deaths will be grouped into a single sub-bullet, while characters that make actual progress will get a morgue.
  • Banners: Each banner that I achieve will get a sub-bullet. Only the highest tier I reach will be included for each banner.

0.31 (January 19, 2024 - February 4, 2024)

This was my first tournament, so I came into it just trying to get a win. I soon realized that with categories and banners, there's a lot more to a tournament than wins. I did manage to get a win, but it was with a build poorly suited for tournament points (caster = more real time taken, low spellpower = more turns taken, Foxfire + Fulminant Prism = more kills). Now that I've done a tournament, I think the key to improvement is to do better in the early game so I'd get more uniques, piety points, and banners. I'd also like to get a melee win, or at least a win with a more points-friendly build. Less than 20% of tournament players get a win, so just getting a win is pretty good by itself, but more deep runs would help.
