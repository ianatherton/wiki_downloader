A huge animated wooden figure. It is highly inflammable.

Useful Info

Wood golems are large constructs made of solid wood. Although immune to Hexes and resistant to most elemental attacks, they have little physical defense and burn easily. They are rarely encountered in the Dungeon, usually in specific vaults.

Tips & Tricks

  • Even without fire damage, their elemental resistances aren't perfect. Elementalists may require a few extra castings to take them down, but they aren't too threatening even then.

History

Wood golems were removed in 0.12.
