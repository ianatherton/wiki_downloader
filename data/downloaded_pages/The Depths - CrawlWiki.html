The Depths are the deepest part of the dungeon proper, subject to the corrupting influence of the Realm of Zot without being considered a part of it.

The Depths are a four-floor branch accessed through a guarded portal on Dungeon:15. Its layout generally resembles that of the Dungeon, although it has a greater tendency toward open spaces and elaborate full floor vaults. Along with several entrances to the Abyss, the Vestibule of Hell, and Pandemonium and an entrance to a Ziggurat, you can find the entrance to the Realm of Zot on Depths:4, guarded by a variety of draconian guards.

Portals

  • A portal to the Abyss always spawns on Depths:3. Other Depths floors have a 33% chance to have a portal.
  • An entrance to the Vestibule of Hell appears on every Depths floor.
  • One ziggurat portal spawns on a random Depths floor.
  • A portal to the Pandemonium always spawns on Depths:2. Other Depths floors have a 25% chance to have a portal.

Strategy

Generally, it isn't advised for you to enter the Depths right after you complete D:15. There are easier areas available (Lair and its branches, Orcish Mines, and The Vaults).
