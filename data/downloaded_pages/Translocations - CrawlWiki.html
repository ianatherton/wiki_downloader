Translocations

From CrawlWiki
Jump to: navigation, search
Version 0.28: This article may not be up to date for the latest stable release of Crawl.

The Translocation school of magic governs the manipulation of the fabric of space. Practitioners gain access to several useful panic buttons, the ability to travel long distances in an instant, and a number of useful offensive and defensive support spells. While its most powerful spells require significant training to acquire, even its level 1 and 2 spells can be extremely useful, making it a popular school for otherwise magic-free brutes to dabble in.
