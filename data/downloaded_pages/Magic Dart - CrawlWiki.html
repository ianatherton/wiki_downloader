Magic Dart is a level 1 Conjurations spell which throws a dart of energy at an opponent, dealing damage. It can never miss or be blocked, but AC reduces its damage like any other attack.

Hedge Wizards and Conjurers begin with this spell memorized.

Strategy

Magic Dart deals less damage than other level 1 spells, but it has excellent range and can't miss. It can get you by the first floor or two. Since it has full LOS range, it can be used to soften up enemies that your other spells (e.g. Searing Ray) can't reach.

Because it only costs 1 MP, Magic Dart has a few other uses:

  • Activating Iskenderun's Battlesphere
  • Detonating ball lightnings early
  • Getting an enemy's attention without shouting yourself (note that the enemy can still shout).

By the mid game, it's impractical to use Magic Dart as a damage spell - the damage is simply too low. You may want to forget it, especially if you aren't using Battlesphere.

Monsters Version

The monster version is similar to the player version, but generally deals more damage.

The following enemies cast Magic Dart:

  • o Blork the orc (3d4 damage)
  • h Natasha (3d4 damage)
  • o Orc wizard (3d4 damage)
  • @ Sigmund (3d4 damage)
