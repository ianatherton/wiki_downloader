Official (Stone Soup)

  • Dungeon Crawl Stone Soup - Official homepage, home of the latest Stone Soup development news, development wiki and bug tracker
  • Tavern 2 - Official forum
  • Tavern - Older forum, sign-ups restricted
  • Crawl-ref-discuss (CRD) - Mailing list
  • Stone Soup Tiles - Now fully integrated into Stone Soup

Official (Pre-Stone-Soup)

  • Linley's Dungeon Crawl - Official homepage of Linley's Dungeon Crawl 4.0
  • crawl-dev - Yahoo! Group that was used as developers' forum, up to and including downloads of the 4.1 alpha [Depreciated]
  • Dungeon Crawl Tile Version - Based on 4.0 with Darshan's patches applied (see below)
  • Darshan's Crawl Page - (Former) Home of the travel patch, which later became one of the foundations of Dungeon Crawl Stone Soup

Spoilers

  • The Knowledge Bot - Information based on the master branch and LearnDB
  • Henzell's LearnDB - Extensive information on almost everything, albeit in a poor format

Outdated Spoilers

  • E. Grasland's Spoilers - Spoilers for most gameplay mechanics (DCSS 0.5.2)
  • Association for the Advancement of Dungeon Crawling - Tutorial, hints and spoilers (LDC 4.0.0b26)
  • ledtim's Crawl calculator - Calculates spell power, damage from racial abilities, spell hunger, healing pacification, god abilities, wands, cards, and rods; also has an enchantment probability calculator; and probability for some HitDice-based resistances. Instructions: Red borders/letters are where you input stuff, and green border/letters are where relevant outputs are. (DCSS 0.9.2)
  • igor's dungeon crawl guides - reviews of various aspects of DCSS.

Discussion

  • The Tavern - Official forum on CDO.
  • rec.games.roguelike.misc - Newsgroup, traditional place to discuss Crawl and other "miscellaneous" roguelikes
  • r/dcss on Reddit - Subreddit focused on DCSS in particular
  • r/Roguelikes on Reddit - Subreddit focused on all roguelikes
  • #crawl - LiberaChat IRC channel. Replaces the old Freenode irc channel
  • Something Awful forums thread - Dungeon Crawl Stone Soup discussion
  • Bay 12 Games Forum thread - Formerly active Dungeon Crawl Stone Soup discussion
  • /vg/ Video Game Generals - Roguelike General thread on 4chan

Let's Play

  • Let's Plays - A list of Dungeon Crawl LPs

Articles

  • Crawl docs - online versions of all documents distributed with Crawl
  • 13 Steps To Crawl - Introductory article
  • Crawling Backwards, Dungeon Crawltrough - Tile-based tutorial [archive]
  • Before Learning to Walk, One Must First Crawl - Roguelike column @Play on Dungeon Crawl [archive]
  • Crawlapalooza Part 1, Skills and Advancement - Roguelike column @Play on Crawl's experience and skill system [archive]
  • Crawlapalooza Part 2: What's With All These Skills, Anyway? - Roguelike column @Play on individual Crawl skills [archive]
  • Crawlapalooza, Part 3: Beogh Liturgical School For Orcs - Roguelike column @Play on some recommended species and job combinations [archive]
  • Crawlapalooza, Part 4: Travel Functions & Play Aids - Roguelike column @Play on Crawl's interface [archive]
  • Sprinting Rapidly Through The Dungeon - Roguelike column @Play on the Crawl variant, Dungeon Sprint [archive]
  • NethackWiki entry - NetHack wiki article on Dungeon Crawl, including a comparison with NetHack
  • RogueBasin - Roguelike development wiki article, including a comparison with other roguelikes
  • Wikipedia entry - Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia, on Linley's Dungeon Crawl
  • Everything2 entry - Linley's Dungeon Crawl at Everything2.com
  • Home of the Underdogs review - Review of Linley's Dungeon Crawl at Home of the Underdogs
  • Abandonia Reloaded review - Review of Linley's Dungeon Crawl at Abandonia Reloaded
  • TvTropes article - TvTropes article on Dungeon Crawl
  • spielendprogrammieren review - Review of stone soup in the spielend-programmieren Blog (in German language)
  • Ubuntuusers.de - detailed installation and compile instruction for Ubuntu Linux (in German language)
  • Cherrypicked bad_ideas - A condensed version of the infamous bad_ideas learndb entry from the ##crawl IRC channel.

Variants

  • Variants - List of variants for Dungeon Crawl Stone Soup.
  • crawlvariations - Home of Zot Defense and Dungeon Sprint, now integrated into Stone Soup
  • hexcrawl - A Dungeon Crawl Stone Soup variant with a hexagonal grid
  • Ax-Crawl - A variant of Linley's Dungeon Crawl
  • Dungeon Crawl Alternative - A variant of Linley's Dungeon Crawl
  • Wii port - Dungeon Crawl Stone Soup for the Nintendo Wii
  • DSCrawl - Dungeon Crawl for the Nintendo DS

Related

  • Captain Pork - Some of Linley Henzell's other games [archive]
  • Temple of the Roguelike - A roguelike news site and blog
  • The RogueBasin Wiki - A roguelike development wiki
  • Ascii Dreams - Unangband development blog, including polls for the roguelike of the year which Dungeon Crawl Stone Soup managed to win in 2008
  • AnyDice - Online calculator for various dice rolls
