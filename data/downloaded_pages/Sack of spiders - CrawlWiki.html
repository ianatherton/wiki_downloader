A woven silk bag. Opening it will cover the area with webs, spiders, and other skittering things. Evocations skill increases spider quality, quantity, and webbing.

A sack of spiders is an evocable item which generates allies themed around the Spider's Nest, and has a chance to create a web on hostile monsters in your line of sight. It can be used once being needing to be recharged with XP. Evocations affects the strength of allies, the chance to web opponents, and number of spiders.

This item is mutually exclusive with the box of beasts. Like with other miscellaneous evocables, only 1 sack of spiders (or box of beasts) will spawn in a given game.

Useful Info

On use, you'll summon 2 plus (2 * Evocations/27 to 4 * Evocations / 27) spiders, randomly rounded.[1]

The following monsters can be created, in ascending order of power:[2]

  • s Scorpion
  • s Redback
  • s Culicivora
  • s Jumping spider
  • s Tarantella
  • s Wolf spider
  • w Steelbarb worm
  • s Demonic crawler
  • s Broodmother

If your god despises a certain creature, it will be replaced with another one.

Summons have a duration of 27 to 171 turns; it does not depend on Evocations skill.

You have a 36 + 2 * Evocations % chance to web a hostile monster not already constricted.[3]

Strategy

At low Evocations, a sack of spiders will often make weak allies... but they can still be useful as meat shields, and a redback or two can be very helpful in the early-game. At high levels of Evocations, it can create powerful allies and consistently web (i.e. net) enemies.

History

  • In 0.31, sacks of spiders were re-introduced and reworked. Prior to this version:
    • They were a consumable item, which you could find multiple in a game. Each use had a 33% chance to consume the sack.
    • You could fail to create anything; Evocations skill decreased the chances.
    • The monster list was significantly different.
  • Sacks of spiders were removed in 0.25.
  • Prior to 0.19, sacks of spiders didn't stack. Instead, each sack of spiders had 5-15 charges.
  • Prior to 0.18, you could summon giant mites, spiders and trapdoor spiders at low Evocations.
  • Prior to 0.15, the webs would be placed randomly, rather than targeting enemies.
  • Sacks of spiders were added in 0.13.
