Earth Magic

From CrawlWiki
Jump to: navigation, search
Version 0.28: This article may not be up to date for the latest stable release of Crawl.

Earth Magic skill determines how easily you can use spells that shape or use earth, stone, iron and other such materials.

Earth Magic spells deal physical damage, having no elemental resistance. Its Conjurations abuse this fact, being single target spells with good damage, but relatively poor accuracy. The school also has a variety of supportive spells, namely Transmutations.
