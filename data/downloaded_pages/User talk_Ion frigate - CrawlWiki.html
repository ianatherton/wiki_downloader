Redirects for alternative capitalizations of article titles

I took a look into this and it seems that mediawiki cannot ignore capitalization for direct links. For example, a link to ghost in the shell will have to be redirected to Ghost in the Shell. You have to make redirects for all the possible capitalizations to the right article..

However, they do have a search function which will ignore capitalization. So if you go into the search bar of wikipedia, type in ghost in the shell, the correctly capitalized article will appear for you to choose. We could ask kornel to implement this functionality.. --Flun 17:27, 23 January 2013 (CET)

Oh well. Thanks for looking though. As for the search functionality, unless I'm mistaken we already have that - I just tried searching for Mirror damage, and it sure enough found the right article, but the redirect doesn't exist yet. -Ion frigate 18:16, 23 January 2013 (CET)
