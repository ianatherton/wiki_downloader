Conjurations

From CrawlWiki
Jump to: navigation, search
Version 0.28: This article may not be up to date for the latest stable release of Crawl.

Conjurations indicates a character's skill at controlling damage-dealing spells of various types. While some spells are pure Conjurations (e.g. Magic Dart), most damage-dealing spells combine this skill with an elemental school or other skill (e.g. Stone Arrow, Hailstorm).

History

  • 0.28 Added Flame Wave. Bolt of Magma is now only for monsters.
  • 0.25 Removed many Conjurations from player spell lists: Flame Tongue, Throw Flame, Throw Frost, Dazzling Spray, Force Lance, Throw Icicle, Bolt of Draining, Venom Bolt, Bolt of Fire, Bolt of Cold, Poison Arrow, and Glaciate. Also added several new spells: Foxfire, Dazzling Flash, Hailstorm, and Starburst.
  • 0.14 Introduced Glaciate to replace Ice Storm.
  • 0.13 Introduced Searing Ray and boosted Force Lance from level 2 to level 5 with a corresponding increase in power.
  • 0.12 Introduced several new, non-elemental Conjurations.
