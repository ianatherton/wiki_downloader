Hexes

From CrawlWiki
Jump to: navigation, search
Version 0.28: This article may not be up to date for the latest stable release of Crawl.

Hexes are spells that affect things and monsters (usually negatively). They mostly ignore dealing direct damage in favour of inflicting debilitating status effects on enemies. Many effects are resisted by willpower, which can be reduced by a scroll of vulnerability or an orb of guile.

History

  • 0.28 Added Enfeeble and Anguish. Invisibility now monster only.
  • 0.20 Moved Portal Projectile into this school and moved Gell's Gravitas out of it.
  • 0.18 The level 5 spell Yara's Violent Unravelling was added to the Book of Hinderance and it was renamed to Book of Misfortune.
  • 0.17 Sure Blade and Enslavement were removed. Darkness was set to level 6 instead of level 7! Gell's Gravitas became level 3 and smite-targeted, but no longer confusing enemies. Silence was now inside Book of Dreams. Metabolic Englaciation was set to level 5.
  • 0.16 Gell's Gravitas was added.
  • Prior to 0.14, you could find Polymorph in the Book of Transfigurations. It caused an effect similar to a wand of polymorph.
  • In 0.8, the Enchantments school was split in order to create Hexes and Charms.
