Useful Info

Mnoleg is a unique Pandemonium lord and guardian of the glowing rune of Zot. Although it has unpredictable melee abilities, it's much more likely to try to kill you through its summoned allies. Mnoleg is accompanied by a horde of neqoxecs, cacodemons, and assorted eyeball-type monsters on its floor of Pandemonium, which has dark red floors and yellow walls.

Tips & Tricks

  • Mnoleg isn't as innately strong as the other Pan lords, but the summons can get out of control. Summon Eyeballs often creates floating eyes, which can inflict paralysis. The other summons are no slouch, either; eldritch tentacles inflict chaotic attacks, while tentacled monstrosities can constrict you in place. Powerful area of effect or piercing attacks are helpful.
    • If you don't have some way of countering floating eyes, be prepared to teleport when you find them.
  • Like all demons, holy and holy wrath damage are extremely effective. Against Mnoleg, silver works just as well.
  • Be prepared to be malmutated while in Mnoleg's realm. A source of mutation resistance is very helpful, if you have one.
  • Mnoleg will be unable to summon eldritch tentacles if you summon one first, via Malign Gateway.

History

  • Prior to 0.27, Mnoleg's spell set included Random Bolt.
  • Prior to 0.19, Mnoleg was considered male.
  • In 0.18, Mnoleg gained Call of Chaos, Random Bolt, and Dig.
  • Prior to 0.17, Mnoleg also had Klown and MR Vulnerability attack flavours.
  • Prior to 0.16, Mnoleg did not have Klown and MR Vulnerability flavoured attacks, but did have Smite.
  • Prior to 0.8, Mnoleg did not have blinking and mutagenic attacks and summoned greater demons instead of eyes. He also had less HP.
  • In early versions of the game, Mnoleg came with two eldritch tentacles attached to him.
