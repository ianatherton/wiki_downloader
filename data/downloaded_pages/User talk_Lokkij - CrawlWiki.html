Even heavy armour users can eventually learn a few useful level 1 or 2 utility spells, but as you've seen, they're not entirely necessary. Apportation, Blink, and Shroud of Golubria are an awesome melee fighter set that even the dumbest brute can pick up. I rarely wind up wearing anything heavier than fire dragon or ice dragon armour in the late game specifically so I can grab a few of those, but if you can find the right resistances elsewhere, it's a matter of taste. --MoogleDan 21:25, 18 April 2013 (CEST)
Hey, congrats on your first win!
I usually find that you can survive without spells if you find the right evocable items and use your god well. Minotaurs aren't entirely useless with Evocations, and if you manage to find good rods/rings/wands/evocable artifacts somewhere, it can help cover some of your bases (though going completely magic-less is always a risk). A good set of crystal plate armour can really help you tank things that you'd otherwise need magic for, and a reasonable stack of javelins lets you soften things up from a distance.
Did you find any scrolls of vorpalize weapon? You can use those to make your own branded weapons, either by using the brand spells or by getting the Blade card from a deck. --spudwalt 01:11, 19 April 2013 (CEST)
