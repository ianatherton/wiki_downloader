Cold is one of the most common elemental forces you will encounter as you explore the Dungeon, consisting of ice-themed and otherwise cool attacks.

Cold Damage

Characters have many ways to gain cold damage. Ice Magic is the most evident, but just about any character can use freezing-branded weapons or a wand of iceblast.

Cold damage is reduced by cold resistance (rC+). Cold-blooded creatures, including player Draconians, are slowed by a few specific cold attacks: weapons of freezing, Freeze, Frozen Ramparts, freezing vapours, Ozocubu's Refrigeration. Having rC+ negates being cold-blooded.

Cold Resistance

The cold resistance intrinsic significantly reduces the cold damage you take when attacked:

Resistance Level Damage Taken (Player) Damage Taken (Monster)
1 50% 50%
2 33% 20%
3 20% 0%

You can acquire cold resistance from a number of sources:

Item

  • Ring of protection from cold and ring of ice: rC+
  • Armour of cold resistance: rC+
  • Gold dragon scales: rC+
  • Ice dragon scales: rC++
  • Staff of cold: rC+
  • Potion of resistance: rC+, temporary
  • Tiamat's dragonskin cloak (50% chance per attack of rC+)
  • Randarts: rC+ to rC+++

Character

  • Being a white draconian: rC+
  • Cold Resistance mutation: rC+/rank
  • The third rank of the Shaggy Fur mutation: rC+
  • The third rank of the Icy Blue Scales mutation: rC+
  • Being a ghoul, mummy, or bloodless vampire: rC+
  • Necromutation: rC+
  • Ice Form: rC+++

Cold Vulnerability

Cold vulnerability causes you to take x150% normal damage from most cold attacks, and x200% damage from freezing melee attacks. You can acquire it by:

  • Wearing a fire dragon scales or ring of fire
  • Dragon Form (except for non-red Draconians)
  • The Cold Vulnerability mutation.
  • Certain unrand artefacts:
    • Amulet of Elemental Vulnerability
    • Salamander hide armour
