Force Lance

For the unrand polearm, see Force Lance (unrand).

Fires a shaft of concussive force. If the impact deals damage, it may knock the target back.

Force Lance is a monster-only spell which deals damage and knocks the target back. The chance to knock back depends on the caster's HD and target size. If the target is a monster, it is briefly stunned (4-9 auts). The maximum distance is 2 + 6 * HD/25 tiles.

If it would knock a creature into impassable terrain (walls, deep water, lava, through a monster), movement is stopped and the target takes impact damage instead. It cannot move stationary monsters like statues or oklob plants.

The following enemies cast Force Lance:

  • p Arcanist (3d14 damage)
  • e Deep elf knight (3d14 damage)
  • v Polterguardian (3d14 damage)
  • R Rakshasa (3d14 damage)
  • N Salamander mystic (3d15 damage)
  • * Wretched star (3d14 damage)

The following enemies may be able to cast Force Lance, depending on their spell set:

  • & Pandemonium lord (3.4% chance)

Strategy

Force Lance can push you off stairs, so be careful when stair dancing. In other cases, the knock back is usually a minor nuisance, though it can mess with your positioning. It can be particularly bad for melee characters, who have to spend turns moving towards the enemy.
