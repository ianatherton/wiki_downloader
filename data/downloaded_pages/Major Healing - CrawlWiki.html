Major Healing

From CrawlWiki
Jump to: navigation, search
Version 0.31: This article is up to date for the latest stable release of Dungeon Crawl Stone Soup.

For Greater Healing, the divine player ability with a similar name, see Elyvilon's page.

Heals a large amount of damage to the caster's body.

Major Healing is a monster-only spell which restores between 50 and 50 + 10 * HD hit points. Note that monsters can cast this spell when they are outside the player's line of sight.

The following enemies cast Major Healing:

  • & Ereshkigal (50-230 damage)
  • & Lom Lobon (50-240 damage)
