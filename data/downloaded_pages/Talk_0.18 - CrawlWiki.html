Remarks on 0.18 developments

I'm rather disappointed by the new ideas of the 0.18 developers. For example: random artifacts (BTW very rare) that destruct themselves when put aside ... horrible! The DCSS game takes a self-destructive course IMHO. I'll probably leave it if it comes even more so. -- Bwijn (talk) 16:18, 7 April 2016 (CEST)

noticed wrong statements

The statement "Natasha, deep elf knights, and salamander stormcallers can no longer cast Conjure Flame." is nonsense. Not even one of the three did have Conjure Flame in its spellset. -- Bwijn (talk) 15:52, 10 May 2016 (CEST)

They did, but those articles were automatically updated when Module:Table of monsters was regenerated for 0.18. That's probably what caused your confusion. - Edsrzf (talk) 23:26, 10 May 2016 (CEST)
It would be nice if content generated from data would include source version somehow. Of course it shouldn't take out much space. -- Medar (talk) 07:05, 11 May 2016 (CEST)
Yeah, right now we're at this strange sort of in-between stage where most monster articles are actually up to date, but the version tags haven't been updated. I would like to improve the state of things somehow; I'm just not sure of the best approach. - Edsrzf (talk) 22:28, 11 May 2016 (CEST)
